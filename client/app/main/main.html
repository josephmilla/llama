LanguaLlama

Real conversations. Real connections. Real education.

LanguaLlama wants to make learning a new language as natural and interactive as possible. Using LanguaLlama will make tackling that language you always wanted to learn a fun and easy task.

The application is designed to connect users to native-speakers of the language they desire to learn, no matter his or her location. Through everyday conversations with individuals, the user will be able to build their language repertoire.

Current language-learning applications don’t utilize peer-to-peer learning like LanguaLlama. Immediate immersion enables the user to build skills and learn every day phrases in a short period of time.This naturalistic approach to learning a foreign language has proven to be the most effective.
